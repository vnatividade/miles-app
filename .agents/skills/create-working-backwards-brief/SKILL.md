# Skill — Create Working Backwards Brief

## Purpose

Create or refine a Working Backwards brief for miles-app.

This skill helps agents clarify product promise, customer problem, strategic assumptions, validation metrics and risks before expanding the MVP.

---

# Required Context

Read:

```txt
product/prd.md
product/feedback-loop.md
product/working-backwards.md, if it exists
execution/execution-map.md
assigned Linear ticket
```

---

# Objective

Produce a product narrative that makes explicit:

- who the product is for
- what painful problem it solves
- why now
- how it works
- what behavior should change
- how success will be measured
- what assumptions need validation
- what risks could kill the idea

---

# Workflow

## 1. Clarify the Customer

Identify:

- target customer
- early adopter profile
- who is explicitly not the customer

---

## 2. Clarify the Problem

Describe the problem in the user's language.

Avoid internal/product jargon.

---

## 3. Define the Promise

Write a concise product promise.

A good promise should be:

- specific
- measurable or observable
- connected to a real pain
- narrow enough for MVP execution

---

## 4. Define the Core Behavior Change

Use:

```txt
From: [current behavior]
To: [desired behavior]
```

---

## 5. Define Metrics

Include:

- north star metric
- activation metrics
- value delivery metrics
- retention metrics
- qualitative signals

---

## 6. Define Strategic FAQ

Answer hard questions:

- What problem are we solving?
- Is it frequent?
- Is it painful?
- What is not MVP?
- Why should we win?
- What could kill the idea?
- What must be learned before scaling?

---

## 7. Define Risks and Assumptions

Map assumptions to validation paths.

Do not treat assumptions as facts.

---

# Important Rules

- Do not write marketing fantasy.
- Do not invent validated demand.
- Distinguish hypothesis from evidence.
- Keep the MVP learning loop central.
- Avoid broad platform language before validation.

---

# Expected Outputs

- working backwards brief
- strategic FAQ
- assumption table
- risk list
- next recommended validation actions
