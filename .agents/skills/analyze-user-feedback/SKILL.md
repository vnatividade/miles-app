# Skill — Analyze User Feedback

## Purpose

Analyze user behavior and feedback signals to determine whether miles-app is delivering perceived value.

---

# Objective

Transform user feedback into:
- product insights
- prioritization signals
- roadmap adjustments
- operational improvements

---

# Signals To Analyze

## Engagement

- active alerts
- recurring usage
- multiple alerts per user
- notification interaction

---

## Satisfaction

- useful/not useful responses
- alert deactivation reasons
- notification complaints
- feedback comments

---

## Product Value

- alerts kept active over time
- opportunities clicked
- alerts resulting in successful ticket finding
- repeated alert creation

---

# Important Rules

## 1. Behavior Matters More Than Opinions

Retention and repeated usage are stronger signals than survey responses alone.

## 2. Negative Feedback Is Strategic

Complaints reveal where matching and relevance fail.

## 3. Track Silent Failure

Users abandoning alerts without explicit complaints is important signal.
